About us

EgyptSLA

is an information technology (IT) services and web design company based in Egypt. Our aim is to provide high-quality and affordable IT and web design services to small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) and individuals. Our services include computer support (hardware and software), establishing and supporting networks, communication systems, data centers, system management and cyber security, ERP software support, website design and development, and light current and monitoring systems.

Our operations will include on-site visits to customers to provide IT and web design services, remote support through remote access software and telephone support, and utilizing our team's expertise in technical support, help desk, system administration, network administration, web development, and light current to provide high-quality services. Our financial projections include calculating the costs associated with starting the business, including equipment, software, and marketing expenses, establishing pricing for the services offered, taking into account the competition and market rates for IT and web design services in Egypt, and projecting the potential revenue for the first year and beyond.

Company Goals

Our goal is to build a reputation as a reliable and trustworthy provider of IT and web design services and to grow the business by expanding the services offered and reaching a larger customer base. Our marketing strategy includes networking and word-of-mouth referrals, advertisements on local classifieds and business directories, and a professional and informative website that showcases our services and allows customers to request support or request a quote for web design services.
